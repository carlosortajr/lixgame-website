Lix

Lix is an action-puzzle game inspired by Lemmings (DMA Design, 1991) with singleplayer puzzles, competitive networked multiplayer, and a level editor. Lix builds on Windows, Linux, and Mac. It's free and open-source software, placed in the public domain via CC0.

Features:

  • Play the Lemmings Forums community pack: 240 singleplayer puzzles.

  • Play NepsterLix and Rubix's levels: 264 extra singleplayer puzzles.

  • Create your own levels: Select "Edit" or "Create Level" in the singleplayer browser.

  • Play networked multiplayer. Who can save the most lix?
